if you want your box to take up all the space of the viewport, setting it to 100% won't work because the height of a page increases as we add content to it and at first it is 0.

10em: 10 x font size of the current element

10rem: 10 x font size of the root element, 16 * 10

why to use em and rem? cause people can manually change the size of font in their browser and with increase in font size, the box will also increase.
